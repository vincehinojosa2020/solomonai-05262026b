- **Lyft Dual Mode:** Church Admins can toggle seamlessly between Admin and Member views via a nav bar switch without logging out or losing session.
- **Mobile-First UX:** Strict adherence to 390px width, minimum 48px tap targets, and ≥16px font sizes to prevent Safari zooming.
