=== PRODUCT OVERVIEW ===
Planning Center is a suite of separate products, each sold individually:
- People: Member database, lists, workflows, forms, directory, background checks
- Services: Worship planning, team scheduling, song library, chord charts, Music Stand
- Check-Ins: Kids/volunteer check-in with label printing (Brother, Dymo, Zebra printers)
- Giving: Online donations, batch entry for cash/checks, pledge campaigns, year-end statements
- Groups: Small group management, leader tools, group chat via Church Center
- Calendar: Room/resource booking, event scheduling, approvals, conflict resolution
- Registrations: Event signups, payments, add-ons, waitlists, assignments
- Publishing: Church Center app customization, custom pages, sermon hosting, themes
- Home: Dashboards for leadership, task management across all PC products

=== HOW PLANNING CENTER WORKS ===
Admin Side: Staff use planningcenter.com for all backend management. Each product has its own admin interface.
Church Center: The member-facing app is called "Church Center" — NOT the church's own brand. Members see "Church Center" branding, not their church name.
Pricing: Each product is priced separately based on usage tiers. A multi-campus church needs to pay for each product across all campuses. Typical cost: $700-$2,400/month for a mid-size church using all products.
Integration: Products share a common People database but have separate interfaces. Switching between products requires navigating to different admin sections.

=== PEOPLE (Member Management) ===
- CSV import for new members (manual field mapping process)
- Profile pages with custom fields, notes, background checks
- Lists: rule-based smart lists and static lists for segmentation
- Workflows: multi-step follow-up processes (e.g., new visitor follow-up, baptism class)
- Forms: collect information from members, auto-create profiles
- Directory: opt-in member directory shared via Church Center
- Automations: trigger actions based on profile changes
- Households: group family members together
- Grade promotion: annual grade updates for children

=== SERVICES (Worship Planning) ===
- Service Types: organize different service styles (Sunday AM, Wednesday Night, etc.)
- Plans: build service order with items (songs, media, custom items)
- Teams: create volunteer teams with positions (Worship Leader, Guitar, Camera, etc.)
- Scheduling: assign volunteers to plans, handle conflicts, send notifications
- Matrix scheduling: visual grid for scheduling across multiple weeks
- Auto-scheduling: automated volunteer assignment based on preferences/blockouts
- Song library: add songs with arrangements, chord charts, files
- Chord Chart Editor: built-in chord chart editing tool
- Music Stand: mobile app for musicians to view charts during rehearsal
- Song integrations: import from CCLI, SongSelect
- Templates: reuse plan structures across weeks
- Keyboard shortcuts for power users
- Signup sheets: let volunteers sign up for open positions
- Secure teams: require background checks for sensitive ministry areas

=== CHECK-INS ===
- Station types: Self (touchscreen kiosk), Manned (staff-operated), Roster (classroom list)
- Label printing: supports Brother (WiFi/Bluetooth/USB), Citizen, Dymo, Zebra printers
- Quick Station: simplified check-in mode for volunteers
- Location hierarchy: campus > building > room with folders
- Time slots: configure check-in windows for different services
- Security codes: parent pickup codes on printed labels
- Reports: attendance tracking by event, location, time
- No geofencing, no QR code check-in (label-based only)
- No AI-powered attendance insights

=== GIVING ===
- Church Center: members donate through the Church Center app or web
- Payment sources: credit card, debit card, ACH bank transfer
- Funds: create designated funds (General, Building, Missions, etc.)
- Batches: manual entry for cash and check donations
- In-kind donations: track non-monetary gifts
- Joint donors: link spouses for combined giving statements
- Pledge campaigns: track commitments against a goal
- Year-end statements: generate tax receipts (PDF, email, or print)
- Refunds and adjustments: modify donation records
- Reporting: giving reports by date range, fund, donor
- No AI giving insights, no giving nudges, no real-time dashboards
- Transaction fees apply (standard credit card processing rates)

=== GROUPS ===
- Group Types: categorize groups (Small Groups, Bible Studies, Serving Teams, etc.)
- Enrollment: open, request-to-join, or invite-only
- Leader tools: leaders manage their group via Church Center
- Events: create recurring group meetings
- Chat: group messaging through Church Center app
- Resources: share files and links with group members
- Tags: organize groups by topic, campus, day of week
- Automations: auto-add people to groups based on conditions
- Campus filtering: multi-campus groups management

=== CALENDAR ===
- Events: create one-time or recurring events
- Rooms & Resources: book shared spaces (Fellowship Hall, Projector, Van, etc.)
- Approvals: request/approve workflow for room bookings
- Conflict resolution: detect double-booked rooms and let admins decide
- Event templates: reuse common event configurations
- Sharing: publish events to Church Center, website embed, or kiosk display
- Import: bring events from external calendars
- Tags: organize events by ministry area

=== REGISTRATIONS ===
- Signups: create events with capacity limits, waitlists
- Selection types: choose options (T-shirt size, meal preference, etc.)
- Add-ons: additional items members can purchase
- Questions/Forms: collect custom information during registration
- Payments: collect fees, apply discounts and scholarships
- Assignments: assign registrants to groups (cabins, teams, etc.)
- Reminders: automated email reminders before events
- Confirmation messages: custom post-registration communication
- Waitlist management: promote from waitlist when spots open

=== PUBLISHING (Church Center App) ===
- Church Center app: the member-facing mobile app (branded as "Church Center", NOT white-label)
- Custom pages: build pages with text, images, buttons
- Navigation: customize the app navigation menu
- Themes: change colors and fonts (limited customization)
- Sermons: host sermon audio/video (basic hosting, no MasterClass-quality experience)
- Page analytics: view page visit statistics
- Plans/pricing: Publishing is a separate paid product

=== HOME (Dashboards) ===
- Dashboards: customizable widgets pulling data from all PC products
- Tasks: assign and track tasks across staff
- Role-based views: different dashboards for executive leadership, worship leaders, children's ministry leaders
- Cross-product visibility: see giving, attendance, and volunteer data in one place
- No real-time war room, no AI insights, no geofencing data
