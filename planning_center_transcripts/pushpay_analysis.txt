PUSHPAY COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS — SOLOMON AI
Source: pushpay.com (scraped March 2026)
Prepared for: Solomon AI competitive positioning

=== COMPANY OVERVIEW ===
Pushpay: "The church engagement platform that grows generosity and deepens connection"
Trusted by: 14,000+ churches (their claim)
Notable Clients: Elevation Church, North Point, Austin Stone, Hosanna
Parent Company: Acquired by BGH Capital (PE firm) — now privately held
Key Stats: "$235M in at-risk donations secured annually" via Everygift technology
Key Differentiator: Full ChMS + Giving + App in one platform (ChurchStaq bundle)

=== PRODUCT SUITE ===
1. Giving (Now powered by Everygift):
   - QuickGive with Apple Pay (under 6 seconds)
   - Everygift: proprietary tech to recover failed transactions, update expired cards
   - AI for Giving Data: ask data questions, get real-time giving insights
   - Tap-to-Give: VisitorTap NFC partnership
   - Stock & Crypto via Engiven partnership

2. ChMS (Church Management Software):
   - AI for People Search
   - Process Queues (automated follow-up)
   - LEAD App (mobile ChMS for staff)
   - Customizable Forms
   - Reporting & Insights
   - SacramentTracker (Catholic-focused)

3. Apps:
   - In-App Giving
   - Groups with Built-In Messaging
   - Volunteer Scheduling
   - Touchless Check-In (pre-check from car, barcode)
   - Sermon Content (live stream + on-demand)

4. Insights:
   - Engagement analytics (giving + attendance + serving)
   - Attendance snapshots (at-risk, new, churned)
   - Giving trends (year-over-year, per-attendee)
   - Serving trends (volunteer engagement)

5. Resi (Video/Streaming):
   - Live streaming platform (separate product/company)
   - AI-powered features for content

=== PRICING STRUCTURE ===
Pushpay does NOT publish specific dollar amounts — "Book a demo" for pricing.
They use 1, 2, and 3-year CONTRACTS (cancellation requires paying contract balance).

Three tiers across each product:
- Core: Essential features
- Advanced: Enhanced tools, integrations, API, branded experience
- Complete: Multi-site, per-campus everything, metric dashboards

Product Combinations:
- Giving only
- Giving + Apps
- ChMS only
- ChurchStaq (Giving + ChMS + Apps + Insights) — their flagship bundle

For Churches vs For Parishes (Catholic-specific features like SacramentTracker)

Payment: ACH auto-debit, monthly or annual
Setup fees: None for plans, optional paid coaching
Check scanning: $0.29/check additional

Key Pricing Intel:
- They require CONTRACTS (1-3 years)
- No public pricing = likely $500-$2,000+/month depending on size
- Industry reports suggest ChurchStaq (full bundle) runs $800-$3,000/mo for mid-large churches
- Transaction fees are not disclosed publicly
