SECUREGIVE COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS — SOLOMON AI
Source: securegive.com (scraped March 2026)
Prepared for: Solomon AI competitive positioning

=== COMPANY OVERVIEW ===
SecureGive: "The complete Donation Management Platform built for the Church"
Founded: ~2006 (20 years in operation as of 2026)
Focus: GIVING ONLY — they are a donation platform, not a full ChMS
Notable Clients: James River Church, Prestonwood Baptist, New Life Church, Celebration Church, Dream City Church, Woodstock
Key Differentiator: Ministry-Minded Processing (effective rate ~2%), giving kiosks, and SecureGive Tap (NFC)

=== PRODUCT SUITE ===
1. Digital Giving (7-in-1): Online, Mobile, Text-to-Give, Kiosk, Check Scanning, Crypto/Stock, Campaigns
2. Donation Management: Dashboard, reporting, donor management, ChMS integrations
3. Church Apps: Custom branded apps ($200-$300/mo extra)
4. SecureGive Tap: NFC tap-to-give hardware (call for quote)
5. Giving Kiosks: iPad ($1,199), Classic ($2,699), Modern ($3,799), Signature ($5,799)

=== PRICING (CONFIRMED FROM WEBSITE) ===
Basic: $149/mo (or $135/mo annual)
  - Online OR Kiosk only (not both)
  - 1 campus, 3 kiosk licenses, 2,000 transactions/mo
  - Donations, purchases, recurring, ACH
  - ChMS manual export + API integration

Premium: $299/mo (or $270/mo annual)
  - Online + Mobile + Text + Kiosk + Check Scanning
  - Up to 5 campuses, 5 kiosk licenses, 2,000 transactions/mo
  - Virtual terminal, auto card updater, API access

Enterprise: Call for quote
  - Everything in Premium + unlimited campuses/transactions/kiosks
  - Crypto, stock, campaigns included
  - Custom transition plan

Add-Ons:
  - Non-Cash Giving: $99/mo
  - Campaigns: $49/mo
  - Church Apps: $200/mo (with SecureGive) or $300/mo (standalone) + $500 setup
  - SecureGive Tap: Call for quote

Transaction Rates:
  - DEBIT: 1.75% + $0.30
  - CREDIT: 2.5% + $0.30
  - Crypto/Stock: 4.0%
  - "Most churches see effective rate of 2% or less"

=== WHAT SECUREGIVE DOES WELL ===
1. 20 years of church giving expertise
2. Physical kiosk hardware lineup (iPad to custom steel)
3. Ministry-Minded Processing rates (competitive but not free)
4. NFC Tap-to-Give (innovative hardware play)
5. 99.999% uptime claim (AWS auto-scaling)
6. Spanish + Portuguese translations
7. Donor covers fees option
8. Auto card updater (reduces failed recurring)
9. No long-term contracts (they advertise this)

=== SECUREGIVE WEAKNESSES (SOLOMON AI ADVANTAGES) ===
1. GIVING ONLY: No member management, no check-in, no groups, no events, no sermons, no volunteers
   → Solomon AI: Full platform replacing 5-6 tools
2. TRANSACTION FEES: 1.75-2.5% + $0.30 per transaction
   → A church processing $100K/mo pays ~$2,500/mo in fees ALONE
   → Solomon Pay vision: lowest fees in industry
3. CHURCH APPS COST EXTRA: $200-$500/mo on top of giving
   → Solomon AI: App included in every plan
4. KIOSK HARDWARE: $1,199-$5,799 per unit
   → Solomon AI: QR code check-in from any phone (no hardware)
5. NO AI: Zero AI capabilities
   → Solomon AI: Ask Solomon AI assistant
6. NO REAL-TIME ANALYTICS: Dashboard updates, not live
   → Solomon AI: War Room with real-time KPIs
7. 2,000 TRANSACTION CAP on Basic/Premium: Forces upgrade
   → Solomon AI: No transaction caps
8. SEPARATE CAMPUS BILLING: Enterprise needed for unlimited
   → Solomon AI: All campuses included in one price
